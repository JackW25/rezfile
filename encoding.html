VIDEO FORMATS

The MP4 is video encoded using H.264 and audio encoded with FLAC

MKV

The MKV is video encoded using H.265 and audio encoded with FLAC
The H.265 encoding is HEVC and may not work with some players, try VLC Media player as an alternative for Windows

AUDIO FORMATS

MP3

The MP3 is downloaded in highest possible quality and encoded with FFMPEG to a MP3

WAV

The WAV is downloaded in highest possible quality and encoded with FFMPEG to a WAV

FLAC

The FLAC is downloaded in highest possible quality and encoded with FFMPEG to a FLAC
